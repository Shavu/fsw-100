Hello fellow adventurers! My name is Jessica, I prefer to go by Jess. I am a mother to 3, and a nature lover at heart. The outdoors is my place to be. I have always felt a connection to nature and find it to be the place I am most inspired, I have a variety of hobbies that branch wildly, but nothing compares to being in the mountains. I cant wait to go back to washington for a beautiful rainy day hike! My dream hike is The Pacific Crest Trail, and one day I hope to achieve it! Above all I just love to learn and enjoy life to the fullest.

Please feel free to leave a comment or share a story, you are welcome to reach out to me as well.
