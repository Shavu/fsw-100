Do you ever get the insatiable sweet tooth when you have nothing on hand? You can easily and quickly make an amazing cake! All you need is the ingredients listed, a mug, and a microwave! This recipe is a huge favorite in my house and if you get cravings for sweets I am sure you will love it too!

This recipe is so fun and easy its a perfect activity to do with your kids. They can feel like little bakers with an amazing cake in a cup to show for it. The flavors are simple and elegant, and the serving portions are perfect! This can also be used as an after school snack or even a late night craving fix!

Ingredients needed

  • 2 tbs(30 grams) Flour
  • 1 tbs(15 grams) unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 tbs(15 grams) sugar
  • a pinch salt
  • 1 tbs(15 grams) butter
  • 2 tbs(30 grams) milk
  • 1/2 tsp(4.34 grams) vanilla extract
  • 1 tbs(15 grams) chocolate chips
  1. melt the butter
  2. in a clean bowl mix the dry ingredients
  3. mix in wet ingredients
  4. microwave for one minute
